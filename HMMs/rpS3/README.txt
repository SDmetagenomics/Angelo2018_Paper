Custom Rps3 (rpsC) HMM

Date: August 14th, 2016

Files:

rpS3_3domains_August2715_noEuk_NR90_min200.fasta.aln -> Muscle alignment of all sequences

HMM Information:
- HMM constructed from 2249 rpS3 sequences spanning Bacterial and Archaeal domains
- Eukaryotic sequences were omitted
- Sequences derived from Hug, et al. 2016
